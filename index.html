How to Use

  1. Connect Your Wallet: Click on the "Connect Wallet" button to connect your Phantom or Solflare wallet.
  2. Enter Token Details: Fill in the fields with the token name, symbol, decimals, and total supply.
  3. Check Your Balance: Ensure that your wallet has at least 0.3 SOL to cover the creation fee.
  4. Create Your Token: Once your wallet is connected and you have sufficient funds, click "Create Token" to create your token.
  5. Transaction Confirmation: You will receive a confirmation message once the token is created successfully!
